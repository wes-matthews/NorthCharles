Cambridge Psychiatric Services, Inc.

Providers pf Psychiatric Recruitment and Placement Services


Cambridge Psychiatric Services, Inc. (CPS) was established in 1983. We specialize in recruiting and matching psychiatrists to the particular staffing requirements of hospitals, clinics, and other mental healthcare providers. CPS recruits for full-time and part-time staff psychiatric positions as well as for locum tenens coverage and for other coverage programs.

Can we help you with your staffing needs? If you are an organization or hospital interested in finding reliable psychiatrists for full or part time positions or even short-term coverage for vacations, maternity or paternity leaves please browse our Organizations Page.

North Charles is there when you need us

For over 35 years North Charles has been providing high quality behavioral healthcare services

We are a behavioral healthcare service provider with program locations in the cities of Cambridge and Somerville Massachusetts. Our Outpatient Mental Health Clinic and our Addiction services counseling center are just outside of Porter Square in Somerville. We also provide research and planning services. In addition, our wholly owned subsidiary, Cambridge Psychiatric Services provides psychiatric moonlighting services at facilities throughout Massachusetts.

In affiliation with the Harvard Medical School Department of Psychiatry at the Cambridge Hospital

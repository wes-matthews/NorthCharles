FEE-FOR-SERVICE CLINICIAN
The North Charles Mental Health Clinic is seeking a fee-for-service clinician. Responsibilities: Diagnostic evaluations, individual psychotherapy and opportunities for family, couples and substance abuse treatment, per applicant training and preferences. Hours: 20+ hours/week, to increase incrementally as caseload expands, and with weekly staff meeting and occasional UR activity (e.g., twice-annual staff “days away”) reimbursed at the same rate. Individual and staff group supervision provided. Job Code: FeeSvcWeb

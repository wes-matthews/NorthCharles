Cambridge Psychiatric Services, Inc.

Providers pf Psychiatric Recruitment and Placement Services


We, at Cambridge Psychiatric Services, maintain an extensive network of carefully screened psychiatrists whose qualifications span many specializations. As mental healthcare professionals ourselves, we work closely with your staff throughout the hiring process to define the position and understand your organization’s needs so that we may refer candidates who will fit into your organization’s style and enhance your clinical staff.

Services

On-Site Coverage
Referral of psychiatrists for night, weekend, and holiday psychiatric coverage for psychiatric work in hospitals, inpatient units and emergency rooms, and consultation services to medical and surgical units.
On-Call Coverage
Referral of psychiatrists for on-call telephone availability with the capacity to come on-site for hospitals and clinics.
Temporary Psychiatric Staffing
Referral of qualified psychiatrists for locum tenens work at hospitals, clinics or other psychiatric practices. Provides coverage for family leaves, vacations, or transitions while recruiting for a permanent position.
Psychopharmacology Clinical Staffing
Referral of psychopharmacology staff to hospitals and clinics for coverage.
Recruitment
Assistance in recruiting qualified psychiatrists for part-time or full-time staff positions.
Program Consultation
Expert program administration consultation to help organizations develop new psychiatric services or refine existing ones.
